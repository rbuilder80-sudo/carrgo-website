Free WooCommerce Plugin: UK Import Readiness Checklist

Running a WooCommerce store and importing stock into the UK? Our free plugin adds a simple import-readiness checklist directly inside your WooCommerce admin. Know exactly what you need — EORI, commodity codes, paperwork — before you ship.

Get Notified at Launch See What It Does

Launching soon on WordPress.org — email support@carrgo.co.uk to be notified or to request a copy. No signup required.

What the Plugin Does

1. Built-In Import Checklist

A clean, one-page checklist inside WooCommerce that tracks:

  • ✅ EORI number status
  • ✅ Commodity code knowledge
  • ✅ Duty and VAT awareness
  • ✅ Commercial invoice readiness
  • ✅ Packing list readiness
  • ✅ Incoterms agreement
  • ✅ Freight booking status

2. Smart Guidance

Answer a few questions and get instant, contextual next steps. Missing a commodity code? We’ll link you straight to the GOV.UK Trade Tariff. No EORI? We’ll show you where to apply.

3. One-Click Quote Request

When you’re ready (or even if you’re not quite sure), hit “Request Carrgo Quote” and get a freight + customs clearance estimate in under 2 minutes.

What’s Included

  • Checklist page inside your WooCommerce admin — track EORI, commodity code and paperwork readiness in one place.
  • Dashboard widget showing your overall import-readiness percentage and suggested next step.
  • Settings page to toggle the dashboard widget and contextual notices on or off.

Who This Is For

  • WooCommerce store owners importing from China, the EU, the USA, or elsewhere
  • Amazon FBA sellers who also run a WooCommerce storefront
  • Dropshippers moving to stock-holding models
  • Small UK ecommerce businesses handling their own imports

How to Get It

At Launch — From WordPress Admin

  1. Go to Plugins > Add New.
  2. Search for “Carrgo UK Import Readiness”.
  3. Click Install Now, then Activate.
  4. Navigate to WooCommerce > Import Check.

Before Launch — Request a Copy

The plugin is not yet listed on WordPress.org. Email support@carrgo.co.uk and we’ll notify you at launch or send you a copy ahead of release. To install manually once you have the ZIP:

  1. Go to Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin.
  2. Choose the ZIP file and click Install Now.
  3. Activate the plugin.

Requirements

  • WordPress 5.8 or higher
  • WooCommerce 5.0 or higher
  • PHP 7.4 or higher

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this plugin really free?

Yes. The checklist plugin is 100% free. We offer freight and customs services if you choose to request a quote, but the plugin itself costs nothing.

When will it be available on WordPress.org?

The plugin is launching soon on WordPress.org and is not yet listed. Email support@carrgo.co.uk to be notified at launch or to request a copy ahead of launch.

Will this slow down my store?

No. The plugin only loads code inside the WordPress admin area. There is zero impact on your storefront or checkout speed.

Do you collect any data?

No. Your checklist answers are stored locally in your WordPress database. We do not collect or transmit any store data unless you explicitly click "Request Carrgo Quote" and fill out our quote form.

Can I suggest new checklist items?

Absolutely. Email us at support@carrgo.co.uk — we read every suggestion.

Does this calculate duty and VAT for me?

Not directly. We link you to the official GOV.UK Trade Tariff tool (https://www.gov.uk/trade-tariff), which is the authoritative source. If you need help classifying your goods, request a Carrgo quote and our customs team can assist.
